# Data Sources Referenced by Data for India (dataforindia.com)

Extracted from ~70 articles across 8 topic areas (Population, Health, Economy, Living Conditions, Work, Law, Climate, Measurement) plus newsletters and explainers. Data for India exclusively uses public data from Indian government agencies and multilateral organizations. Sources are deduplicated and organized by category.

---

## 1. Indian Government Household Surveys

### Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS)
- **Link:** https://microdata.gov.in/NADA/index.php/catalog/213/related-materials
- **Publisher:** National Statistical Office (NSO)
- **What:** India's primary annual labour force survey (successor to EUS, from 2017-18 onwards). Covers ~400,000+ respondents. Records employment status, worker classification (self-employed/salaried/casual), earnings, unemployment, LFPR, sectoral distribution, education levels, vocational training. Uses both Usual Status (365-day) and Current Weekly Status approaches.
- **Use cases:** Employment trends, women's workforce participation, graduate employment, self-employment analysis, manufacturing workforce, salaried jobs, vocational training, literacy rates, education levels. The single most referenced data source across all DFI articles.

### National Family Health Survey (NFHS)
- **Link:** https://www.nfhsiips.in/nfhsuser/index.php
- **Publisher:** International Institute of Population Sciences (IIPS) for Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
- **What:** Large-scale multi-round household survey (5 rounds since 1992). Covers 637,000+ households on fertility, family planning, maternal/child health, nutrition, immunisation, household amenities, biomarkers, asset ownership. Part of the global DHS program.
- **Use cases:** Childhood immunisation, infant mortality, child stunting, anaemia prevalence, maternal mortality, sanitation access, electricity access, vehicle ownership, dairy/meat consumption, sex ratio analysis, fertility rates. Second most referenced source.

### Household Consumption Expenditure Survey (HCES)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/sites/default/files/publication_reports/Report_591_HCES_2022-23New.pdf
- **Publisher:** National Sample Survey Office (NSSO)
- **What:** India's primary national survey on household spending, conducted every ~5 years since 1972. Covers 500+ items (food, clothing, shelter, durables, utilities). Most recent: 2022-23.
- **Use cases:** Consumption expenditure trends, poverty analysis, dairy/meat spending, vehicle ownership, electricity usage patterns, drinking water sources, inflation adjustment baselines.

### Comprehensive Annual Modular Survey (CAMS / NSS Round 79)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/sites/default/files/publication_reports/CAMS%20Report_October_N.pdf
- **Publisher:** NSSO (2022-23)
- **What:** Covers 1 million+ households on drinking water, sanitation, housing, mobile phone/internet access, banking access, literacy.
- **Use cases:** Current drinking water access, phone/internet usage, ICT skills, banking access, rural-urban digital divide.

### Sample Registration System (SRS)
- **Link:** https://censusindia.gov.in/census.website/data/SRSB
- **Publisher:** Office of the Registrar General & Census Commissioner, India
- **What:** Large-scale continuous demographic survey tracking births, deaths, and fertility. Covers ~8.8 million people using dual-record system. Produces annual estimates of life expectancy, IMR, fertility rates, and causes of death (via verbal autopsy).
- **Use cases:** Life expectancy estimates, infant/neonatal mortality, fertility rates (TFR), maternal mortality ratio, cause-of-death analysis (disease transition), road accident mortality estimation, malaria mortality.

### Employment and Unemployment Surveys (EUS)
- **Publisher:** NSSO (1972 to 2011-12)
- **What:** Historical employment surveys conducted roughly every 5 years. Predecessor to PLFS.
- **Use cases:** Long-run employment trends, structural shift from agriculture, historical female LFPR, baseline comparisons.

### Census of India
- **Link:** https://censusindia.gov.in/
- **Publisher:** Registrar General of India
- **What:** Decennial population census (last conducted 2011). Covers literacy, education, demographics, housing, population counts by every administrative unit.
- **Use cases:** Historical baseline for literacy, education, population data. Sampling frame for other surveys. All articles note no census since 2011.

### NSO Education Surveys (Multiple Rounds)
- **Link:** https://microdata.gov.in/NADA/index.php/home
- **Publisher:** National Statistics Office
- **What:** Five education-focused surveys from 1980s to 2018 (Rounds 52, 64, 71, 75, 78, 79). Covers enrollment, dropout reasons, education levels.
- **Use cases:** Long-term education trends, dropout analysis, pre-PLFS education data.

### India Human Development Survey (IHDS)
- **Publisher:** NCAER and University of Maryland
- **What:** Major national panel survey covering income, health, education, development indicators.
- **Use cases:** The only major national survey estimating income (vs. consumption).

### All India Debt and Investment Survey (AIDIS)
- **Publisher:** National Statistics Office (2019)
- **What:** National household survey on financial product ownership (credit/debit cards) with demographic breakdowns.
- **Use cases:** Financial inclusion analysis, gender gaps in card ownership.

## 2. Population and Demographic Data

### UN World Population Prospects (WPP)
- **Link:** https://population.un.org/wpp/
- **Publisher:** United Nations Population Division
- **What:** Global demographic database with historical data from 1950 and projections to 2100. Includes life expectancy, fertility, age structure by country.
- **Use cases:** Life expectancy comparisons (India vs UK), fertility projections, demographic dividend analysis, working-age population projections.

### Population Projections (Census 2011-based)
- **Link:** https://mohfw.gov.in/sites/default/files/Population%20Projection%20Report%202011-2036%20-%20upload_compressed_0.pdf
- **Publisher:** Registrar General & Census Commissioner / National Commission on Population
- **What:** State-level population projections through 2036 based on 2011 Census.
- **Use cases:** Converting survey percentages to absolute population numbers (used in nearly every DFI article). Now outdated since no census since 2011.

### SRS Abridged Life Tables
- **Link:** https://censusindia.gov.in/census.website/data/SRSALT
- **Publisher:** Registrar General of India
- **What:** Annual estimates of life expectancy across overlapping 5-year periods, with sex/age-specific death rates, rural/urban breakdowns.
- **Use cases:** Rural vs urban life expectancy, inter-state variations, gender-based mortality trends.

### UN-DESA International Migrant Stock
- **Link:** https://www.un.org/development/desa/pd/content/international-migrant-stock
- **Publisher:** UN Department of Economic and Social Affairs
- **What:** International migrant stock data by country of birth/citizenship. Sourced from destination country censuses.
- **Use cases:** Indian emigrant population (18.5 million in 2024), destination country distribution. The only available source since India doesn't maintain official emigrant data.

## 3. Health Data Sources

### WHO Global Health Observatory
- **Link:** https://www.who.int/data/gho/
- **Publisher:** World Health Organization
- **What:** Global health indicators, definitions, and estimates -- IMR, MMR, road traffic mortality, malaria, anaemia thresholds, health workforce density.
- **Use cases:** International health benchmarks, indicator definitions, recommended targets (e.g., 44.5 health workers per 10,000).

### IHME Global Burden of Disease (GBD)
- **Link:** https://www.healthdata.org/research-analysis/gbd
- **Publisher:** Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation, University of Washington
- **What:** Comprehensive modeled estimates of disease burden, causes of death, and health metrics globally. Combines government registries, DHS surveys, and studies.
- **Use cases:** Cause-of-death among neonates/infants, malaria incidence estimates (5.5M vs government's 3.4 lakh), homicide rate estimates.

### Comprehensive National Nutrition Survey (CNNS) 2016-18
- **Link:** https://nhm.gov.in/WriteReadData/l892s/1405796031571201348.pdf
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Health and Family Welfare
- **What:** Nationally representative survey of 100,000+ children/adolescents aged 0-19. Covers nutritional biomarkers including anaemia (venous blood method).
- **Use cases:** Child stunting evidence, anaemia prevalence comparison with NFHS, iron deficiency levels.

### Million Death Study (MDS)
- **Publisher:** Registrar General of India / CGHR
- **What:** Large survey (2007-2014) within SRS using "verbal autopsy" to identify causes of death.
- **Use cases:** Immediate causes of maternal deaths, regional variations in cause-of-death patterns.

### NCVBDC (National Centre for Vector Borne Diseases Control)
- **Link:** https://ncvbdc.mohfw.gov.in/
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
- **What:** Government surveillance data on malaria cases (passive and active) including Annual Parasite Incidence.
- **Use cases:** Official malaria case reporting (though significantly underestimates vs modeled estimates).

### DHS STATcompiler
- **Link:** https://www.statcompiler.com/en/
- **Publisher:** DHS Program (USAID)
- **What:** International comparative health survey data across 90+ countries.
- **Use cases:** Cross-country stunting rate comparisons, immunisation coverage benchmarking.

### ICMR-NIN Dietary Guidelines for Indians (2024)
- **Link:** https://nin.res.in/dietaryguidelines/pdfjs/locale/DGI_2024.pdf
- **Publisher:** Indian Council of Medical Research - National Institute of Nutrition
- **What:** Nutritional recommendations for dairy and food group intake by age group.
- **Use cases:** Daily dairy intake recommendations context.

## 4. Economic and Financial Data

### RBI Database on Indian Economy (DBIE)
- **Link:** https://data.rbi.org.in/
- **Publisher:** Reserve Bank of India
- **What:** Official RBI data repository -- NSDP/GSDP, payment systems indicators, balance of payments, currency statistics.
- **Use cases:** State economies analysis, retail payments trends, trade deficit calculations.

### RBI Payment Systems Indicators
- **Link:** https://data.rbi.org.in/DBIE/#/dbie/reports/Statistics/Financial%20Sector/Payment%20Systems
- **Publisher:** Reserve Bank of India
- **What:** Monthly volume and value data for all payment modes (NEFT, UPI, debit/credit cards, cheques, cash).
- **Use cases:** Digital payment adoption trends, UPI transaction analysis, shift from cheques to digital.

### RBI Basic Statistical Returns (BSR)
- **Link:** https://data.rbi.org.in/#/dbie/reports/Publication/Time-Series%20Publications/Basic%20Statistical%20Return%20%28BSR%29-2
- **Publisher:** Reserve Bank of India
- **What:** Deposit statistics from all scheduled commercial banks.
- **Use cases:** Banking access analysis (nearly 2.5 billion bank accounts held by individuals).

### National Accounts Statistics (MoSPI)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation
- **What:** National GDP, GVA by sector, per capita income, provisional estimates.
- **Use cases:** GDP analysis, manufacturing GVA, sectoral composition, unorganised manufacturing contribution.

### Annual Survey of Industries (ASI)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/annual-survey-industries
- **Publisher:** National Statistics Office (conducted since 1960)
- **What:** Comprehensive survey of factory data -- employment, ownership, industry classification, expenditure, output value, capital formation.
- **Use cases:** Factory counts (250,000), employment figures (18.5M workers), manufacturing hubs analysis, women in factory employment.

### Consumer Price Index (CPI)
- **Publisher:** MoSPI (collected from 1,181 rural and 1,114 urban markets)
- **What:** Monthly index tracking prices of 299 items. Base year 2012.
- **Use cases:** Inflation measurement, real wage calculations, poverty line adjustments.

### Labour Bureau Wage Data
- **Publisher:** Labour Bureau, Government of India
- **What:** Monthly wage data for rural agricultural workers by occupation and gender.
- **Use cases:** Real wage impact of inflation on agricultural laborers (2014-2024).

### IMF World Economic Outlook
- **Link:** https://www.imf.org/en/Publications/WEO
- **Publisher:** International Monetary Fund
- **What:** Global GDP projections and economic forecasts for all countries.
- **Use cases:** India's GDP ranking projections, state economy comparisons with countries.

### World Bank World Development Indicators
- **Link:** https://databank.worldbank.org/source/world-development-indicators
- **Publisher:** World Bank
- **What:** Comprehensive global development database -- poverty, health, education, trade, infrastructure indicators for all countries.
- **Use cases:** International comparisons across nearly every topic (poverty rates, vaccination coverage, road mortality, drinking water, electricity access, trade ratios, graduate unemployment).

### World Bank Global Findex Database
- **Link:** https://www.worldbank.org/en/publication/globalfindex
- **Publisher:** World Bank
- **What:** Survey of 140,000+ respondents across 135+ countries on bank account ownership, card usage, and financial inclusion.
- **Use cases:** Indian bank account ownership (80%), debit/credit card penetration, gender gaps in account activity.

### World Bank Poverty and Inequality Platform (PIP)
- **Link:** World Bank PIP portal
- **What:** Global poverty estimates, poverty lines, inequality measures. Uses PPP conversion factors.
- **Use cases:** India poverty rate calculations, international poverty line methodology.

### International Comparison Programme (ICP)
- **Link:** https://www.worldbank.org/en/programs/icp
- **Publisher:** World Bank
- **What:** Calculates Purchasing Power Parities (PPPs) across countries.
- **Use cases:** Converting national poverty lines to comparable international standards, per capita income comparisons.

## 5. Trade and Industry Data

### DGCIS (Directorate General of Commercial Intelligence and Statistics)
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **What:** India's official source for merchandise/goods trade statistics.
- **Use cases:** Import/export values for goods ($850B imports, $780B exports).

### Export Import Data Bank
- **Link:** https://tradestat.commerce.gov.in/
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **What:** Detailed commodity-level trade data using HS codes.
- **Use cases:** Specific import values for semiconductors, displays, electronics.

### PPAC (Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell)
- **Link:** https://ppac.gov.in/
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas
- **What:** Petroleum imports, exports, consumption, production statistics.
- **Use cases:** India's petroleum import dependence (90% of crude oil imported).

### National Industrial Classification (NIC) 2008
- **Link:** https://www.ncs.gov.in/Documents/NIC_Sector.pdf
- **Publisher:** Central Statistics Office
- **What:** Official industry classification system for India.
- **Use cases:** Classifying factories by sector, categorizing manufacturing activities, tracking custom tailoring classification changes.

## 6. Education Data

### All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE)
- **Link:** https://aishe.gov.in/aishe-final-report/
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Education
- **What:** Higher education enrollment and institutional data (post-Class 12). Covers 40 million enrollment, discipline/gender breakdowns.
- **Use cases:** Higher education enrollment trends, gender distribution across disciplines, postgraduate statistics.

### UDISE Plus (Unified District Information System for Education)
- **Link:** https://dashboard.udiseplus.gov.in/#/home
- **Publisher:** Department of School Education and Literacy, Ministry of Education
- **What:** Pre-primary through Grade 12 school education data -- enrollment, dropout rates, infrastructure.
- **Use cases:** School enrollment trends, dropout analysis, educational infrastructure tracking.

### ASER (Annual Status of Education Reports)
- **Link:** https://asercentre.org/aser-survey/
- **Publisher:** ASER Centre / Pratham
- **What:** Learning outcomes, functional literacy, and numeracy skills among school-age children in rural India (since 2005).
- **Use cases:** Functional literacy gaps (86% Grade 8 reading at Grade 2 level in 2005, declining to 70% by 2022).

## 7. Crime and Legal Data

### National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB)
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Home Affairs
- **What:** Annual crime reports since 1953 -- FIR-based incident counts, murder/homicide rates, victim/accused demographics, motive analysis.
- **Use cases:** Homicide statistics, crime rates per 100,000 population, gender breakdowns.

### e-Courts Portal / DDL Judicial Data
- **Link:** https://services.ecourts.gov.in/ / https://www.devdatalab.org/judicial-data
- **What:** 43 million+ case records from subordinate courts (2010-2020).
- **Use cases:** Most commonly invoked civil/criminal laws, court workload analysis.

### National Judicial Data Grid
- **Link:** https://njdg.gov.in/
- **What:** Dashboard tracking pending civil and criminal cases at district court level.
- **Use cases:** Judicial backlog context (11M civil + 36.5M criminal cases pending).

## 8. Climate and Infrastructure Data

### India Meteorological Department (IMD)
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Earth Sciences
- **What:** High-resolution gridded temperature data (1901-2024) from ~700 stations, 500+ manual observatories, 1,000+ Automatic Weather Stations. Daily min/max/mean temperatures.
- **Use cases:** Temperature trend analysis, climate anomalies relative to 1991-2020 baseline, national temperature records.

### Energy Statistics India (NSO)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/sites/default/files/publication_reports/EnergyStatistics_India_publication_2024N.pdf
- **What:** Annual electricity production (1.4M GWh), domestic household consumption statistics.
- **Use cases:** National electricity generation baseline, household usage proportions.

### Ministry of Road Transport & Highways Annual Reports
- **Link:** https://morth.nic.in/
- **What:** Police-reported road accident statistics -- accidents, deaths, injuries, traffic violations.
- **Use cases:** Official road accident mortality (150,000+ deaths in 2022), rural/urban distribution.

### TRAI (Telecom Regulatory Authority of India)
- **What:** Active mobile SIM registrations, telecom subscriber data.
- **Use cases:** Mobile SIM growth (346M in 2008 to 1B+ in 2024).

### ITU (International Telecommunication Union)
- **Link:** https://www.itu.int/en/ITU-D/Statistics/
- **What:** Global mobile phone penetration rankings, SIM statistics, internet usage rankings.
- **Use cases:** India ranked 136th of 162 in mobile penetration, ~100th of 160 in internet usage.

### WHO/UNICEF Joint Monitoring Programme (JMP)
- **Link:** https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/sanitation
- **What:** Global sanitation and drinking water definitions and statistics.
- **Use cases:** 2000 baseline (14% of India had basic sanitation), comparative sanitation progress.

### Annual Report: Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation
- **Link:** Ministry of Jal Shakti
- **What:** Administrative data on tap water connections to rural households.
- **Use cases:** Government tap water connection coverage (60%+ of rural households by mid-2023).

### Vehicle Registration Data (Ministry of Road Transport & Highways)
- **What:** Vehicle registration statistics from regional transport offices.
- **Use cases:** 260 million two-wheelers, 50 million cars registered; vehicle type composition.

### Income Tax Department Time Series Data
- **Link:** https://www.incometaxindia.gov.in/documents/d/guest/final-time-series-data-pdf
- **What:** Historical statistics on income tax return filings.
- **Use cases:** Only 81 million individuals (8% of working-age population) filed ITR in 2024-25.

## 9. International Labour and Development Data

### ILO / ILOSTAT
- **Link:** https://ilostat.ilo.org
- **Publisher:** International Labour Organization
- **What:** Global labour statistics -- employment, unemployment, working conditions across countries. Modeled estimates for cross-country comparison.
- **Use cases:** International employment comparisons (India vs China, Sri Lanka, US), manufacturing workforce gender comparisons, unpaid care work measurement.

### ILO India Employment Report 2024
- **Link:** https://www.ilo.org/sites/default/files/2024-08/India%20Employment%20-%20web_8%20April.pdf
- **What:** Analysis of employment quality, job growth, educated youth labor market behavior.
- **Use cases:** Graduate unemployment causes and job market dynamics.

### IPCC AR6 Reports
- **Link:** https://www.ipcc.ch/report/ar6/
- **What:** Global temperature trends, climate change projections, warming impacts.
- **Use cases:** Global warming context (1.1C above pre-industrial), heat intensity relationships.

## 10. Additional Sources (from Measurement explainers and later agents)

### Civil Registration System (CRS)
- **Link:** https://censusindia.gov.in/census.website/data/VSREPORT
- **Publisher:** Office of the Registrar General, India
- **What:** Continuous, mandatory registration of all births, deaths, and stillbirths. Registration completeness: 98.4% births, 97.2% deaths (2023). Raw unit-level data NOT publicly available.
- **Use cases:** Death registration tracking, vital statistics baseline. DFI explains CRS in detail in their CRS-SRS explainer.

### Medical Certification of Cause of Death (MCCD)
- **Link:** https://censusindia.gov.in/nada/index.php/catalog/45565
- **Publisher:** Office of the Registrar General
- **What:** Medically certified cause-of-death data using ICD-10 classification. Only ~22% of Indian deaths are medically certified.
- **Use cases:** Cause-of-death analysis where available. DFI notes MCCD covers only about 1 in 5 deaths.

### Annual Survey of Unincorporated Sector Enterprises (ASUSE)
- **Link:** MoSPI
- **Publisher:** National Statistics Office
- **What:** Survey of informal/unincorporated sector enterprises and self-employed workers.
- **Use cases:** Demand-side data for unorganised manufacturing employment, informal sector analysis.

### Time Use Survey (TUS)
- **Publisher:** Government of India
- **What:** Survey tracking how individuals allocate time across activities including work, domestic duties, and leisure.
- **Use cases:** Recommended as complement to PLFS for capturing women's unpaid economic participation.

### Urban Frame Survey (UFS)
- **Link:** https://mospi.gov.in/
- **Publisher:** NSSO
- **What:** Updated sampling frame for urban areas, measuring urban area characteristics and affluence levels.
- **Use cases:** Sampling design for HCES and PLFS urban strata.

### Comprehensive Modular Survey - Telecom (CMS-T) 2025
- **Link:** https://microdata.gov.in/NADA/index.php/catalog/239
- **Publisher:** National Statistics Office
- **What:** Survey of 34,250 respondents on ability to use computers and smartphones.
- **Use cases:** Digital literacy measurement (~200 million adults can operate a computer as of 2025).

### NITI Aayog SDG India Index
- **Link:** https://www.niti.gov.in/
- **What:** India's progress report on Sustainable Development Goals, tracking indicators across states.
- **Use cases:** LPG/PNG connections vs actual usage tracking, aspirational districts benchmarking.
