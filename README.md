# Credible and Useful Data Sources for India

Combined, deduplicated report from analysis of **XKDR Forum** (xkdr.org) and **Data for India** (dataforindia.com). Covers 59 XKDR research papers, 15+ blog articles, and ~70 DFI articles across 8 topic areas.

Individual reports: [XKDR Report](xkdr_data_sources.md) | [DFI Report](dfi_data_sources.md)

---

## A. Indian Government Household Surveys & Census

These are the foundational data sources for understanding India. Both organizations rely heavily on these.

### 1. Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS)
- **Link:** https://microdata.gov.in/NADA/index.php/catalog/213/related-materials
- **Publisher:** National Statistical Office (NSO), Ministry of Statistics
- **What:** India's primary annual labour force survey (from 2017-18). Covers 400,000+ respondents on employment status, worker classification, earnings, unemployment, LFPR, sectoral distribution, education levels, vocational training, literacy.
- **Use cases:** Employment trends, women's workforce participation, graduate employment, self-employment, manufacturing workforce, salaried jobs, vocational training, literacy rates, education levels.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (nearly every Work, Economy, and Living Conditions article), XKDR (health worker analysis)

### 2. National Family Health Survey (NFHS)
- **Link:** https://www.nfhsiips.in/nfhsuser/index.php
- **Publisher:** IIPS for Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
- **What:** Large-scale multi-round household survey (5 rounds since 1992). Covers 637,000+ households on fertility, family planning, maternal/child health, nutrition, immunisation, household amenities, biomarkers, asset ownership. Part of the global DHS program.
- **Use cases:** Childhood immunisation, infant mortality, child stunting, anaemia, maternal mortality, sanitation, electricity access, vehicle ownership, dairy/meat consumption, sex ratio, fertility, banking access.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (nearly every Health and Living Conditions article), XKDR (health research)

### 3. Household Consumption Expenditure Survey (HCES)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/ (report for 2022-23)
- **Publisher:** NSSO
- **What:** National survey on household spending, every ~5 years since 1972. Covers 500+ items. Most recent: 2022-23 (155,000+ households).
- **Use cases:** Consumption expenditure trends, poverty analysis, food spending patterns, vehicle ownership, electricity usage, inflation adjustment, real income analysis.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Economy, Living Conditions, Health articles), XKDR (poverty and household analysis)

### 4. Sample Registration System (SRS)
- **Link:** https://censusindia.gov.in/census.website/data/SRSB
- **Publisher:** Office of the Registrar General & Census Commissioner
- **What:** Continuous demographic survey covering ~8.8 million people. Tracks births, deaths, fertility via dual-record system. Includes verbal autopsy for causes of death.
- **Use cases:** Life expectancy, infant/neonatal mortality, fertility rates (TFR), maternal mortality ratio, cause-of-death analysis, disease transition, road accident/malaria mortality estimation.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (all Population and most Health articles), XKDR (health policy papers)

### 5. Census of India
- **Link:** https://censusindia.gov.in/
- **Publisher:** Registrar General of India
- **What:** Decennial population census (last conducted 2011). Covers literacy, education, demographics, housing, population counts.
- **Use cases:** Historical baseline for all demographic analysis. Sampling frame for other surveys. Both orgs note no census since 2011 is a major data gap.
- **Referenced by:** Both organizations extensively

### 6. Comprehensive Annual Modular Survey (CAMS / NSS Round 79)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/sites/default/files/publication_reports/CAMS%20Report_October_N.pdf
- **Publisher:** NSSO (2022-23)
- **What:** Covers 1 million+ households on drinking water, sanitation, housing, phone/internet access, banking, literacy.
- **Use cases:** Current drinking water access, phone/internet usage, ICT skills, digital divide analysis.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Living Conditions, Measurement articles)

### 7. Population Projections (Census 2011-based)
- **Link:** https://mohfw.gov.in/sites/default/files/Population%20Projection%20Report%202011-2036%20-%20upload_compressed_0.pdf
- **Publisher:** Registrar General / National Commission on Population
- **What:** State-level projections through 2036 based on 2011 Census.
- **Use cases:** Converting survey percentages to absolute numbers. Used in nearly every article on both sites.
- **Referenced by:** Both organizations extensively

### 8. Employment and Unemployment Surveys (EUS)
- **Publisher:** NSSO (1972 to 2011-12)
- **What:** Historical employment surveys, predecessor to PLFS.
- **Use cases:** Long-run employment trends, structural shift from agriculture, historical female LFPR.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Work articles), XKDR (historical context)

## B. Economic and Financial Data

### 9. National Accounts Statistics / GDP (MoSPI)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation
- **What:** National and state GDP, GVA by sector, per capita income, provisional estimates.
- **Use cases:** GDP analysis, manufacturing GVA, sectoral composition, state economy comparisons.
- **Referenced by:** Both organizations

### 10. Annual Survey of Industries (ASI)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/annual-survey-industries
- **Publisher:** NSO (since 1960)
- **What:** Comprehensive factory data -- employment, ownership, industry classification, expenditure, output, capital formation. Covers 250,000 factories.
- **Use cases:** Factory counts, manufacturing employment (18.5M workers), manufacturing hubs, women in factory employment.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Economy articles)

### 11. RBI Database on Indian Economy (DBIE)
- **Link:** https://data.rbi.org.in/
- **Publisher:** Reserve Bank of India
- **What:** Official RBI data -- NSDP/GSDP, payment systems, balance of payments, banking statistics, currency data.
- **Use cases:** State economies, retail payments, digital payment adoption, trade analysis, banking access.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Economy articles)

### 12. Consumer Price Index (CPI)
- **Publisher:** MoSPI (1,181 rural + 1,114 urban markets)
- **What:** Monthly index tracking prices of 299 items. Base year 2012.
- **Use cases:** Inflation measurement, real wage calculations, poverty line adjustments.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Economy articles), XKDR (fiscal analysis)

### 13. CMIE Databases (Prowess, CPHS, States of India, Economic Outlook)
- **Link:** https://www.cmie.com/ (commercial subscription)
- **Publisher:** Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy
- **What:** Suite of databases -- Prowess (corporate), CPHS (200,000 household panel), States of India (state macro indicators), Economic Outlook (macro data).
- **Use cases:** Financial distress prediction, household savings, electricity expenditure, state fiscal analysis, trade data, corporate research.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR (multiple papers). Note: DFI explicitly does not use private data sources.

### 14. GSDP / State Domestic Product
- **Link:** https://mospi.gov.in
- **Publisher:** MoSPI
- **What:** State-level GDP in nominal and real terms.
- **Use cases:** Property tax buoyancy analysis, state fiscal comparisons, development benchmarking.
- **Referenced by:** Both (DFI for state economies, XKDR for fiscal research)

## C. International and Multilateral Sources

### 15. World Bank World Development Indicators (WDI)
- **Link:** https://databank.worldbank.org/source/world-development-indicators
- **What:** Comprehensive global database -- poverty, health, education, trade, infrastructure for all countries.
- **Use cases:** International benchmarking across every topic. Both organizations use it extensively for cross-country comparisons.
- **Referenced by:** Both organizations

### 16. IMF World Economic Outlook (WEO)
- **Link:** https://www.imf.org/en/Publications/WEO
- **What:** Global GDP projections and economic forecasts.
- **Use cases:** India's GDP ranking, PPP comparisons, state economy comparisons with countries.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Economy articles)

### 17. UN World Population Prospects (WPP)
- **Link:** https://population.un.org/wpp/
- **What:** Global demographic database (1950 to 2100 projections).
- **Use cases:** Life expectancy, fertility projections, demographic dividend, working-age population trends.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Population articles)

### 18. WHO Global Health Observatory
- **Link:** https://www.who.int/data/gho/
- **What:** Global health indicators, definitions, and estimates.
- **Use cases:** IMR/MMR definitions, health workforce thresholds, road mortality, malaria, anaemia.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Health articles)

### 19. ILO / ILOSTAT
- **Link:** https://ilostat.ilo.org
- **What:** Global labour statistics, modeled employment estimates, working conditions.
- **Use cases:** International employment comparisons, manufacturing gender ratios, unpaid care work.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Work articles)

### 20. IHME Global Burden of Disease (GBD)
- **Link:** https://www.healthdata.org/research-analysis/gbd
- **What:** Modeled estimates of disease burden and causes of death globally.
- **Use cases:** Neonatal cause-of-death, malaria incidence (5.5M modeled vs 3.4L official), homicide rates.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Health articles)

### 21. World Bank Global Findex Database
- **Link:** https://www.worldbank.org/en/publication/globalfindex
- **What:** Survey of 140,000+ across 135+ countries on bank/card usage and financial inclusion.
- **Use cases:** Indian bank account ownership (80%), card penetration, gender gaps.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Economy articles)

### 22. World Bank International Comparison Programme (ICP)
- **Link:** https://www.worldbank.org/en/programs/icp
- **What:** Purchasing Power Parities across countries.
- **Use cases:** PPP-adjusted income comparisons, poverty line conversions.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Economy articles)

### 23. DHS STATcompiler
- **Link:** https://www.statcompiler.com/en/
- **What:** Cross-country comparative health survey data from 90+ countries.
- **Use cases:** Stunting, immunisation, and health indicator comparisons.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Health articles)

### 24. UN-DESA International Migrant Stock
- **Link:** https://www.un.org/development/desa/pd/content/international-migrant-stock
- **What:** International migrant stock data by country of birth/citizenship.
- **Use cases:** Indian emigrant population tracking (18.5M in 2024).
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Population articles)

## D. Judicial and Legal Data

### 25. e-Courts Database
- **Link:** https://services.ecourts.gov.in/ecourtindia_v6/
- **What:** Public database of court records across Indian district courts -- filing details, hearing dates, disposal status, order copies.
- **Use cases:** Court performance evaluation, litigation duration analysis, case lifecycle research, MACT analysis.
- **Referenced by:** Both organizations

### 26. National Judicial Data Grid (NJDG)
- **Link:** https://njdg.ecourts.gov.in/
- **What:** Aggregated judicial statistics on pending cases across Indian courts.
- **Use cases:** Judicial backlog analysis (47+ million pending cases), case counting methodology critique.
- **Referenced by:** Both organizations

### 27. Development Data Lab (DDL) Judicial Data Portal
- **Link:** https://www.devdatalab.org/judicial-data
- **What:** 43 million+ case records from subordinate courts (2010-2020).
- **Use cases:** Identifying most commonly invoked laws, court workload analysis.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Law articles)

### 28. NCRB (National Crime Records Bureau)
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Home Affairs
- **What:** Annual crime reports since 1953 -- FIR-based incident counts, crime rates, demographics.
- **Use cases:** Homicide statistics, crime rates per 100,000, gender breakdowns.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Law articles)

### 29. XKDR Legal System Dataset
- **Link:** https://xkdr.github.io/legal-system-dataset-frontend/
- **What:** Life-cycle data for commercial cases (Bombay HC) and IBC cases (NCLT Mumbai), 2022-2024. Open dataset.
- **Use cases:** Court reform research, litigation duration analysis.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR (open dataset)

### 30. FRG Insolvency Cases Dataset
- **Link:** http://ifrogs.org/dms/IBC/nclt_data.html
- **What:** Hand-collected bankruptcy cases under IBC from NCLT/NCLAT orders.
- **Use cases:** Financial distress prediction, insolvency research.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR (finance papers)

## E. Climate, Energy, and Satellite Data

### 31. India Meteorological Department (IMD)
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Earth Sciences
- **What:** Gridded temperature data (1901-2024), 700+ stations, daily min/max/mean. 500+ manual observatories, 1,000+ AWS.
- **Use cases:** Temperature trends, climate anomalies, national temperature records.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Climate article)

### 32. VIIRS Nighttime Lights (Earth Observation Group)
- **Link:** https://eogdata.mines.edu/products/vnl/
- **What:** Monthly satellite imagery of nighttime radiance. April 2012 onwards, 15-arc-second resolution.
- **Use cases:** Economic activity proxy, property tax analysis, war impact measurement.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR (multiple papers + NighttimeLights.jl package)

### 33. NASA POWER Data (Solar Irradiance)
- **Link:** https://power.larc.nasa.gov/data-access-viewer/
- **What:** Satellite-derived solar irradiance data by location.
- **Use cases:** Solar panel sizing for agricultural solarisation.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR (Tamil Nadu solarisation paper)

### 34. CEA All India Electricity Statistics
- **Link:** https://cea.nic.in/
- **What:** National electricity generation, installed capacity, energy sources.
- **Use cases:** Non-fossil fuel energy share, electricity sector context.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR (electricity papers)

### 35. Energy Statistics India (NSO)
- **Link:** https://www.mospi.gov.in/ (annual publication)
- **What:** Annual electricity production (1.4M GWh), consumption by sector.
- **Use cases:** National electricity baseline.
- **Referenced by:** DFI (Living Conditions articles)

### 36. CMIE CapEx (Project-Level Infrastructure Database)
- **Link:** https://capex.cmie.com/ (commercial subscription)
- **Publisher:** Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy
- **What:** Project-level database tracking investment projects from announcement through implementation and completion. Contains 8,540+ electricity generation projects (1996-present), covering capacity, ownership (public/private), location, project status (announced, implemented, completed, stalled, shelved), cost estimates, and timeline milestones. Updated quarterly from public filings, company contacts, and news sources.
- **Use cases:** Infrastructure project completion analysis, energy capacity pipeline forecasting, capital expenditure tracking, stalled project monitoring, state-level project comparison.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR/TrustBridge (Borah, Jaitly & Sane 2026 -- "Evaluating India's Energy Ambitions"), widely used in infrastructure policy research.
- **Note:** Distinct from CMIE Prowess/CPHS. The CapEx derivative "CapEx dx" provides aggregated quarterly investment data. Both require commercial subscription.

### 37. MERIT India (Merit Order Dispatch)
- **Link:** https://meritindia.in/
- **Publisher:** Ministry of Power / POSOCO
- **What:** Real-time and historical data on power plant dispatch, marginal costs, and generation by plant. Covers all major thermal, hydro, nuclear, and renewable stations.
- **Use cases:** Plant-level generation analysis, merit order economics, renewable curtailment tracking, electricity market analysis.

### 38. Global Energy Monitor -- India Trackers
- **Link:** https://globalenergymonitor.org/ (open access)
- **Publisher:** Global Energy Monitor
- **What:** Project-level trackers for solar, wind, coal, gas, and nuclear power plants globally, including India. Covers project status, capacity, location (coordinates), developer, and commissioning dates. Includes the Global Solar Power Tracker, Global Wind Power Tracker, and Global Coal Plant Tracker.
- **Use cases:** District-level energy project mapping, coal-to-renewable transition tracking, cross-referencing with CMIE CapEx for project validation.

## O. Additional Notable Sources (from complete agent results)

### 64. GHSL Building Volume Data (European Commission JRC)
- **Link:** https://ghsl.jrc.ec.europa.eu/
- **What:** Satellite-derived building volume grids at ~90m resolution globally.
- **Use cases:** Property tax potential estimation (explains >80% of PT demand variation).
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 65. Mahabhulekh / State Land Records Portals
- **Link:** https://mahabhulekh.maharashtra.gov.in (Maharashtra)
- **What:** Digitised parcel-level land records (7/12 extracts) with ownership, area, land type.
- **Use cases:** Women's land ownership mapping, land record quality analysis.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 66. NCAER Land Records and Services Index (N-LRSI)
- **What:** Index measuring land record digitisation across 33 states.
- **Use cases:** Studying link between land record quality and household credit access.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 67. MCA-21 Database (Ministry of Corporate Affairs)
- **Link:** https://www.mca.gov.in/
- **What:** Financial filings of ~1.1 million registered companies.
- **Use cases:** MSME analysis, firm-level research.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 68. NSE/BSE Market Data (Tick-by-tick)
- **What:** Microsecond-level trading data from Indian stock exchanges.
- **Use cases:** Market microstructure research, algorithmic trading analysis.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 69. RBI Handbook of Statistics (Multiple Tables)
- **Link:** https://dbie.rbi.org.in/
- **What:** Ownership of government securities, interest rates, maturity patterns, CRR/SLR data.
- **Use cases:** Analysis of sovereign debt composition and who lends to the Indian state.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 70. EPFO / PFRDA / IRDAI Annual Reports
- **What:** Investment portfolio data for pension, provident, and insurance funds.
- **Use cases:** Tracking institutional investor mandatory government bond holdings.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 71. Civil Registration System (CRS)
- **Link:** https://censusindia.gov.in/census.website/data/VSREPORT
- **What:** Mandatory registration of births, deaths, stillbirths (98%+ completeness by 2023).
- **Use cases:** Vital statistics baseline, mortality tracking.
- **Referenced by:** DFI

### 72. Detailed Demands for Grants (DDGs) / CGA Accounts
- **Link:** Ministry websites / https://cga.nic.in/
- **What:** Object-head expenditure data from all Union government ministries.
- **Use cases:** Estimating government procurement (11-12% of GDP), spending gap analysis.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 73. Crime Victimisation Surveys (India)
- **What:** CHRI (2015), IDFC/SATARC (2017), Karnataka CVS (2018-19), SPIR.
- **Use cases:** Measuring actual vs reported crime, non-reporting patterns.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 74. Harmonized Nighttime Light Dataset (Li et al.)
- **Link:** https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.9828827.v7
- **What:** Combined DMSP-OLS + VIIRS annual nighttime lights (1992-2021).
- **Use cases:** Long-run economic activity analysis.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 75. Global Data Lab Subnational HDI
- **Link:** https://globaldatalab.org
- **What:** Subnational socioeconomic indicators including GNI per capita.
- **Use cases:** Ground-truth for nighttime lights GDP proxy validation.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR

### 73. State GIS Portals / ULB Boundary Shapefiles
- **Link:** https://kgis.ksrsac.in/ (Karnataka), https://gis.rajasthan.gov.in/ (Rajasthan)
- **Publisher:** State GIS agencies (KSRSAC for Karnataka, RSAC for Rajasthan, etc.)
- **What:** GIS shapefiles of administrative boundaries -- ULB (Urban Local Body) limits, district/taluk/village boundaries. Available for some states; coverage is uneven. Karnataka's KGIS portal provides downloadable ULB boundary shapefiles used in property tax research.
- **Use cases:** Clipping satellite rasters (GHSL, VIIRS) to municipal boundaries for fiscal capacity estimation. Overlaying spatial data with municipal finance records. Essential for any ULB-level geospatial analysis.
- **Referenced by:** XKDR (Seth et al., 2026 -- property tax potential paper)

### 74. Survey of India Administrative Boundaries
- **Link:** https://onlinemaps.surveyofindia.gov.in/
- **Publisher:** Survey of India, Department of Science and Technology
- **What:** Official administrative boundary maps and shapefiles for India -- international, state, district, sub-district, and village boundaries. The authoritative source for India's geospatial boundary data. Also see community alternatives: DataMeet (https://github.com/datameet/maps), ML Infomap, and OpenStreetMap extracts.
- **Use cases:** Base layer for any spatial analysis requiring administrative boundaries. District/sub-district level aggregation of satellite or survey data.
- **Referenced by:** Multiple research contexts

### 75. DLC Rates / ePanjiyan (State Property Valuation Portals)
- **Link:** https://epanjiyan.rajasthan.gov.in/ (Rajasthan), https://igrs.ap.gov.in/ (AP), https://igr.maharashtra.gov.in/ (Maharashtra)
- **Publisher:** State Departments of Registration and Stamps (IGRS)
- **What:** District Level Committee (DLC) rates / circle rates / guideline values -- the government-notified minimum property valuations for each locality, used as the floor for stamp duty calculation and property registration.
